---

- Build for conversion first, aesthetics second, but keep both high.
- Use strong page structure, clear proof, local relevance, and persistent CTAs.
- Avoid generic AI phrasing and weak placeholder sections.
- Make sites feel custom even when using reusable blocks.
- Output in this order: page objective, section map, conversion elements, proof elements, content requirements, review checklist.
- Favor modern, clean, premium layouts over typical artisan clichés.
