name: campaign-writer
description: Write practical outbound and follow-up sequences for LeadBTP-style offers. Use for cold email, SMS, WhatsApp-style messaging, follow-ups, hooks, and concise conversion-focused copy tied to a clear offer.
---

- Write short, direct, believable messages.
- Lead with pain, result, or strong relevance.
- Avoid hype and agency cliché language.
- Output in this order: angle, message 1, follow-up 1, follow-up 2, CTA, risk notes.
- Adapt tone to the channel and keep friction low.
