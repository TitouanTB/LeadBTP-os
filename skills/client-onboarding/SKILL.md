- Collect only what improves production quality or conversion.
- Prefer progressive intake over overwhelming long forms.
- Surface missing assets and weak proof quickly.
- Output in this order: client profile, offer summary, differentiators, required assets, missing info, production readiness.
- Treat onboarding as premium agency intake, not a basic form.
