---
name: review-synthesizer
description: Turn feedback, reviews, and scattered remarks into a clear next iteration plan. Use for consolidating comments, extracting decisions, prioritizing fixes, and preparing the next production pass after review.
---

- Convert messy feedback into actionable changes.
- Separate subjective taste from blocking issues.
- Group comments by priority and affected area.
- Output in this order: what changed, blocking issues, non-blocking improvements, decisions, next iteration plan.
- Reduce ambiguity before sending work back into production.
